MY STORY

When I got cancer when I was 26, my whole world collapsed. After my treatment and the dreaded black hole, I looked for a place where I could pick up life at my own pace, in a safe environment and with a warm and understanding community. Unfortunately I couldn't find that anywhere so I decided to create it for myself and everyone who needs it.

Founder, Stefanie Veraghtert
OUR COFFEE

The Big C. works with freshly roasted organic coffee beans from a traditional coffee roaster in Belgium. By buying this coffee you not only enjoy a delicious "slow" coffee, but you also contribute to supporting and connecting people affected by cancer or another serious illness. For every 1 kilo of coffee sold, a contribution of 1 € goes to this goal.

OUR CONCEPT

In addition to being a warm meeting place, our coffee bars are also a special place where you can get social and economic opportunities for your personal development and social reintegration. We work with sustainable products that contribute to a healthy world and we always put people first.
